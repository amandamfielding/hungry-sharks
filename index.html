WELCOME TO HUNGRY SHARKS!

How to play:
Click a shark to feed it a fish while keeping track of which sharks you have already fed and which are still hungry. If you feed the same shark twice, you lose. Feed each shark once to move on the next level. With each level, another shark is added.

Rules:
The player must wait three seconds in between feedings. The timer will let you know when you can feed again.
